General

We work with a broad range of institutions: K-12 schools, colleges and universities, public libraries, NGOs, corporations, research institutes, and government agencies worldwide. If your organization needs structured access to African knowledge resources, we have a plan for you.

All institutional licenses include full access to our Knowledge Archive, Digital Library, and curriculum resources. Certain premium or specially commissioned content may require supplementary licensing — our team will clearly outline what's included when preparing your quote.

Pricing & Plans

Pricing is based on three factors: your institution type, the number of concurrent users, and the plan tier you select. We apply volume discounts automatically — institutions with 10+ users receive 5–20% off depending on scale. Use our pricing calculator for an instant estimate, or contact us for a customized quote.

Access & Integration

Absolutely. Licenses can be configured for multi-campus deployments using IP range authentication, proxy server support (EZProxy compatible), or single sign-on (SSO) via SAML 2.0. Our technical team handles the full onboarding setup at no extra charge.

Yes. We provide API access and LTI connectors for integration with popular LMS platforms including Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, and D2L Brightspace. API documentation and dedicated integration support are included in Professional and Enterprise plans.

Support & Onboarding

On-campus users are authenticated automatically via IP recognition. Remote access is available through your institution's proxy server or by logging in with an institutional email address. We also support federated identity providers for seamless off-campus access.

Support levels scale with your plan. Starter includes email support with 48-hour response times. Professional adds live chat and priority queuing. Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager, phone support, and scheduled training sessions for your staff and librarians.
